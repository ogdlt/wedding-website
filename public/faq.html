What to wear

Dress code

Friday Cocktail attire — elegant but relaxed. Think summer evening style: linen or lightweight suits, shirts and jackets, cocktail dresses, midi dresses, or tailored sets. As we’ll be in El Saler in July, breathable fabrics for an outdoor setting are recommended
Saturday Formal attire. We invite men to wear a suit and tie, and women to choose a midi dress or similarly elegant formalwear. We kindly ask guests not to wear white or similar colors.
Note There will be no need for heel protections for women, as the venues are flat and the ground is covered evenly.
