Travel & Stay

Everything you need to organise your trip, accommodation and transport for the wedding weekend.

Accommodation

A limited number of rooms have been reserved at Parador El Saler for guests travelling from outside Valencia. We will contact the relevant guests directly with the booking details.

For guests staying elsewhere, we recommend booking accommodation either in central Valencia or near El Saler, depending on your plans for the weekend.

Where to Stay

Guests who are not staying at Parador El Saler may find it most convenient to stay in central Valencia, where there is a wide range of hotels and easy access to transport for the weekend.

If you prefer to stay closer to the beach and the Sunday plans, the El Saler area is also a good option.

Transport

For the wedding day on Saturday, buses will depart at 16:30 from Parador El Saler and from central Valencia.

Return buses from the venue will leave at 01:00 and 03:00.

For Sunday, please indicate in your RSVP whether you plan to attend the pool party and whether you require bus transport.

Travel to Valencia

Valencia is easily accessible by air and train. Guests arriving from outside Spain may find Valencia Airport the most convenient option, while guests travelling from within Spain may also consider high-speed rail connections.

More detailed recommendations can be added here later if you would like to include airport, train station or transfer information.
